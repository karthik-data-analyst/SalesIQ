# SalesIQ 📊
### AI-Powered Sales Analytics Platform

## What is SalesIQ?
SalesIQ analyzes large sales datasets and generates 
AI powered business insights automatically inside 
a live Power BI dashboard.

## The Problem it Solves
Business owners have thousands of rows of sales data 
but no time to analyze it. They make decisions based 
on gut feeling instead of data — losing revenue every 
month.

## How it Works
1. Python cleans and processes raw sales data
2. AI analyzes key business metrics automatically
3. Power BI shows 5 interactive visual dashboards
4. AI insights update live when user filters by 
   region, category, or time period

## Dashboard Visuals
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Category
- Profit by Region
- Top 10 Customers
- Live AI Business Insights

## Sample AI Output
Technology has highest profit margin at 15.61%

Action: Expand technology product line immediately
Central region underperforming vs West by 44%

Action: Review Central region sales strategy
November is peak sales month

Action: Prepare inventory 6 weeks before November

## Tech Stack
- Python
- Groq AI API (LLaMA 3.3 70B)
- Pandas
- Power BI Desktop
- Matplotlib

## Dataset
9,994 rows of real retail sales data
Categories: Furniture, Technology, Office Supplies
Regions: East, West, Central, South
